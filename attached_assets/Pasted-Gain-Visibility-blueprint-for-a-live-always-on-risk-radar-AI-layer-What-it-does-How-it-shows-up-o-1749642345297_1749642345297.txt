What the user actually sees
Command-centre map — colour-coded world map with drill-down to supplier clusters.

Live incident feed — real-time ticker of ESG events, sanctions additions, reputational spikes; filters by severity, country, or category.

Supplier passport — side-panel card with CRI trend, ESG sub-scores, latest documents, and linked AP/AR exposure.

Risk backlog — kanban board of open mitigations, owner avatars, SLA clock.

One-click report — instant PDF or XLS extract for auditors, investors, or internal risk committees.

Together these elements turn the Gain Visibility pillar into a true “radar and flight deck”: you know where risk is forming, why it matters, and who is driving the fix—before it hits your cash or compliance scorecard.
