End-to-end example
Detect – AI spots $15 m tied up because 120 customers usually pay 10 days late; model predicts 70 % will accept 1 %–10-day discount.

Simulate – Discount optimiser shows $10 m net liquidity gain vs status quo; scenario engine confirms resilience under a 150 bp rate hike.

Execute – Platform emails dynamic-discount offers and creates SCF options for suppliers; collections bot focuses reps on the 30 highest-risk invoices.

Monitor – Dashboard tracks real cash unlocked, re-scores behaviour, and fine-tunes offers weekly.

Why it matters to the CFO
Immediate cash release: 1–3 % of annual revenue freed within 6–12 months.

Lower financing cost: Smart early-pay earns double-digit equivalent yield while preserving supplier health.

Stronger relationships: Data-driven terms feel fair—driving higher supplier participation and faster customer payments.

Bottom line: by focusing AI on just two levers—Accounts Receivable and Accounts Payable—your finance team can continuously convert trapped working capital into strategic cash, without touching inventory.
