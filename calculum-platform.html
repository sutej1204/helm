Process flow

Five steps in ADA’s AI-driven analysis

Data Import and Data Cleansing

You can easily import your spend data using APIs or via a secured, fully encrypted data feed from your Excel and CSV files. There is no need for any integration or IT involvement from your side. The process is fast and only requires a minimum of six data points.

Data Normalization

ADA’s AI engine normalizes your supplier data, allowing you to easily use the correct information on each company. The ADA Platform matches over 83% of all our clients' suppliers and over 92% of their spend volume.

Data Enrichment

Information aggregates from multiple trusted data sources and providers with the most accurate and up-to-date information on millions of businesses worldwide. This includes financial data, industry & commodity information, ESG & sustainability dat, and many more data points.

Data Categorization & Anonymization

ADA auto-categorizes your suppliers and your spend using AI to accurately classify them using market-standard taxonomies. The self-learning engine allows you to quickly correct any missed classifications for future analysis. All data is fully anonymized in the ADA Platform.

Data Analysis

ADA structures the data and selects the appropriate algorithms to analyze and interpret the data into actionable results. Data is presented to the user by applying our award -winning data visualization platform. ADA applies different scenarios and allows input from the user, creating a feedback loop.
