Build an interactive web app mockup for the “Working Capital Optimisation” module within Rotar — an AI-native supply chain intelligence platform.

The page should be designed for procurement, treasury, and finance users. It must showcase how businesses can unlock working capital and reduce risk by adjusting supplier payment terms and accessing embedded financing solutions like payables financing and receivables factoring.

Key Features to Include:
1. Term Optimisation Engine
Upload or simulate supplier payables data (e.g., JSON or sample table)

Visualize suppliers by current terms (30/60/90 days), size, risk rating, and strategic tier

Provide AI-driven recommendations to extend payment terms where viable

Slider to simulate cash flow impact of extending terms across the supplier base

2. Receivables Financing Panel
Display AR ledger with buyers, invoice values, payment dates, and credit scores

Select invoices for non-recourse factoring

Show advance rate, discount rate, and funding cost

Calculate improvement to DSO and liquidity released

3. Payables Financing Tool (replaces Dynamic Discounting)
Show list of suppliers eligible for early payment via third-party funding (Orbian-style)

For each supplier, display:

Invoice value

Proposed early pay date

Applicable discount/funding cost

Effective APR

Simulate executing the early payment using external funding vs internal cash

Output cash flow impact and supplier adoption analytics

4. Scenario Simulator
Users adjust DSO, DPO, and % of invoices financed

View real-time impact on:

Free cash flow

Net working capital

Supply chain risk exposure

Include “Generate Board Report” button to export a PDF summary

5. Action Execution Panel
Simulate workflows to:

“Extend Terms & Notify Suppliers”

“Request Payables Financing”

“Finance Receivables”

Mock API console output showing backend logic (for demo purposes)

Design Considerations:
Clean, enterprise-grade UI using Tailwind CSS or ShadCN

Focus on actionable financing decisions, not just analytics

Show how Rotar bridges the gap between data and funding

Goal:
Showcase how Rotar enables businesses to turn supplier data into financing strategy—allowing CFOs and treasury teams to unlock cash, reduce cost of capital, and enhance resilience—with Orbian as the embedded funding rail.
